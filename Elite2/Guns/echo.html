Overview

The Echo CS-10 is a clip system Nerf blaster that was released in fall of 2020 under the Elite 2.0 series for 30 dollars

Specifications

  • Feet Per Second: 79 FPS
  • Ammo Type: Standard Elite
  • Capacity: Expandable (Mag Fed)
  • Firing Mechanism: Springer
  • Accessories: Stock, 10 dart mag, and Barrel

Features

The Echo is a slide-action clip system blaster similar to the N-Strike Elite Delta Trooper and Retaliator. It advertises itself as a "4-in-1" blaster, due to its compatibility with detachable barrel extensions and shoulder stocks. Despite the attachments both having tactical rails on them, the blaster itself has none. The Echo is also capable of being slam-fired.

Personal Experience

One of our personal favorite blasters as it rarely jams, has an acceptable range, and has many tactical rails
