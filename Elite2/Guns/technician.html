Overview

The Technician DB-2 is a double barrel Nerf blaster that was released in 2021 under the Elite 2.0 series. It comes packaged in the Loadout Pack blaster set and cannot be bought separately. This pack is sold for 20 dollars.

Specifications

  • Feet Per Second: 72 FPS
  • Ammo Type: Standard Elite
  • Capacity: 2 Bullets
  • Firing Mechanism: Springer
  • Accessories: None

Features

The Technician DB-2 is a single-fire pump-action blaster similar to the Alpha Strike Tiger DB-2. It has a unique open dart chamber between the muzzle and the actual barrels of the blaster. Both barrels use an intelligent air restrictor to allow a single dart to fire from either one. It is compatible with detachable shoulder stocks. It features a single tactical rail on top and has a sling point on the heel of its grip.
